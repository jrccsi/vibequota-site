VibeQuota is designed to help users monitor and understand their AI tool usage across supported services. This Privacy Policy explains what information VibeQuota collects, how that information is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Information We Collect

VibeQuota is intended to collect only the information necessary to operate the app, sync usage information across your devices, support purchases, improve reliability, and provide customer support.

Usage Data You Choose to Track

VibeQuota may collect and store usage-related information about supported AI tools, such as Claude and Codex. This may include information such as usage counts, limits, reset times, quota status, timestamps, and related app state needed to display usage information in the app, menu bar, and widgets.

VibeQuota does not intentionally collect the content of your prompts, responses, files, source code, conversations, or other materials you submit to AI services.

Device and App Information

VibeQuota may collect limited technical information needed to operate and improve the app, such as app version, operating system version, device type, crash information, diagnostic logs, performance information, and error events.

Purchase Information

If you purchase a paid version, lifetime unlock, or other in-app purchase, purchase and subscription status may be processed through Apple and RevenueCat. This may include information such as product identifier, transaction status, entitlement status, purchase date, renewal or expiration status, and related purchase metadata.

VibeQuota does not collect or store your full payment card number. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store.

iCloud Sync Data

If iCloud sync is enabled, VibeQuota may store app data in your private iCloud account using Apple’s iCloud services. This allows your usage status and related app state to sync between your Apple devices.

Support Communications

If you contact us for support, we may collect the information you provide, such as your email address, message content, screenshots, logs, or other details you choose to send.

2. How We Use Information

We use information collected by VibeQuota to:

  • Provide and operate the app.
  • Display usage status, quotas, limits, reset times, notifications, and widgets.
  • Sync app data across your devices when iCloud sync is enabled.
  • Manage purchases, trials, and paid entitlements.
  • Diagnose crashes, errors, and performance issues.
  • Improve app reliability, functionality, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Protect against misuse, fraud, or abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Analytics and Diagnostics

VibeQuota may use analytics and diagnostic tools to help understand app performance, identify crashes, detect errors, and improve reliability. These tools may process technical information such as app version, device type, operating system version, crash logs, performance events, and general app usage events.

Where possible, analytics and diagnostic information is limited, minimized, and not intended to include the contents of your AI prompts, responses, files, source code, or conversations.

4. Third-Party Services

VibeQuota may use third-party service providers to operate the app and related services. These may include:

  • Apple for App Store distribution, in-app purchases, iCloud, CloudKit, and device services.
  • RevenueCat for purchase validation, entitlement management, and subscription or lifetime unlock status.
  • Sentry for crash reporting and error diagnostics.
  • PostHog for product analytics and app usage insights.

These third-party providers may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms. We use these services only to support app functionality, purchases, diagnostics, analytics, and reliability.

5. iCloud and Local Storage

VibeQuota may store certain app data locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, some app data may also be stored in your private iCloud account so it can sync across your devices.

Data stored in iCloud is handled by Apple under Apple’s iCloud and privacy practices. You can manage iCloud availability and app access through your Apple device settings.

6. Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

VibeQuota is not designed to collect:

  • The text of your AI prompts.
  • AI-generated responses.
  • Source code or files you submit to AI tools.
  • Passwords or authentication credentials for AI services.
  • Payment card numbers.
  • Health, financial, biometric, or government identification information.

If you voluntarily send sensitive information to us in a support request, we will use it only as needed to respond to your request and will take reasonable steps to limit further use.

7. Data Sharing

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app, process purchases, provide diagnostics, or perform analytics.
  • With Apple or RevenueCat as necessary to validate purchases and manage entitlements.
  • When required by law, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of users, the app, or our business.
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, support purchases, maintain security and reliability, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Usage data stored locally on your device remains under your control unless synced through iCloud. You may delete app data by using available app controls, disabling iCloud sync, deleting iCloud app data, or uninstalling the app.

Purchase records may be retained as needed to maintain entitlement status, comply with App Store requirements, support refunds or purchase restoration, and meet legal or accounting obligations.

9. Your Choices and Controls

Depending on your device settings and app configuration, you may be able to:

  • Delete the app from your device.
  • Delete locally stored app data.
  • Disable iCloud sync for the app.
  • Manage iCloud data through Apple settings.
  • Restore or manage purchases through the App Store.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of support-related information you provided directly to us.

Some information, such as App Store purchase history, is managed by Apple and may need to be handled through your Apple account settings.
